  <h1>Simple Pancakes</h1>
  <p>Fluffy and easy pancakes ready in under 20 minutes!</p>

  <ul>
    <li>1 cup flour</li>
    <li>1 tablespoon sugar</li>
    <li>2 teaspoons baking powder</li>
    <li>1 cup milk</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Instructions</h2>
  <ol>
    <li>Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.</li>
    <li>Add milk and stir until just combined.</li>
    <li>Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat.</li>
